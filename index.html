Who Is Daniel Cullen

Industry Leader. Coach. Family Man.

Daniel J. Cullen is a Wisconsin business executive with close to twenty years of experience in construction and manufacturing. As Director at Precision Metal Fab Inc. in Delafield, he oversees strategic planning, talent development, and the key investments that keep the company competitive in Wisconsin's miscellaneous metals market.

Daniel's leadership philosophy is straightforward: find great people, give them what they need, and trust them to deliver. That same people-first approach shapes everything he does — from running boxing sessions at Rock Steady Boxing in Janesville to coaching youth basketball for the Waukesha South Blackshirts.

Away from work and the gym, he is a devoted husband and proud father of four, an active catechist at St. Anthony's on the Lake, and a published author. Learn more about Daniel Cullen's full background and community work, or explore his writing below.
